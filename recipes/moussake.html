Moussake

Description

Aubergine with sauce and covered in cheese.

allrecipes.com says: 'This moussaka tastes great! Layers of sliced aubergine are baked in a ground beef sauce and smothered in a creamy white béchamel sauce in this classic Greek dish.'

Ingredients

  • 3 aubergines, peeled and cut lengthwise into 1/2 inch thick slices.
  • salt to taste
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 4 cups milk

Steps

  1. Melt butter
  2. Stir in beef, onion and seasoning
  3. Add herbs and sauce
  4. Scald milk in pan
  5. Whisk flour and butter
  6. Pre-heat Oven
  7. Arrange aubergine in baking tray
  8. Cover aubergines with meat sauce and sprinkle cheese on top
